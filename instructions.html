Memory Card Game

Game Instructions

Game Goal

The goal of the game is to find all matching pairs of cards. Each card has a hidden value, and the player must remember the positions of the cards to make matches.

How to Play

  1. Enter your player name on the launcher page.
  2. Select the board size and difficulty level.
  3. Choose optional features such as hints or timer.
  4. Click Open Game Window.
  5. Click Start Game.
  6. Click on a card to flip it over.
  7. Click a second card to try and find a matching pair.
  8. If the cards match, they remain visible.
  9. If they do not match, they flip back after a short delay.
  10. Continue until all pairs are matched.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: fewer cards, longer time before cards flip back
  • Medium: moderate number of cards and timing
  • Hard: more cards and faster flip-back time

Game Controls

  • Start Game begins a new game
  • Show Hint temporarily reveals a card or pair
  • Save Session saves your current progress
  • Load Session restores a saved game
  • Reset Game clears the current game
  • Back to Settings returns to the launcher page

Scoring

  • Each correct match increases your score
  • Fewer moves result in a better score
  • Faster completion may give bonus points
  • Using hints may reduce your score

Tips

  • Try to remember the positions of cards you have already seen
  • Work systematically across the board
  • Use hints sparingly to maximise your score
